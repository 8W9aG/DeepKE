Convert a one-dimensional array representing sequence length to a two-dimensional mask, the default position of the pad is 1. Convert 1-d seq_len to 2-d mask. :param seq_len: shape will be (B,) :type seq_len: list, np.ndarray, torch.LongTensor :param max_len: Pad the length to this length. The default (None) uses the longest length in seq_len. But in the scenario of nn.DataParallel, the seq_len of different cards may be different, so you need to pass in a max_len so that the length of the mask is from pad to that length. :type max_len: int

Returns

shape will be (B, max_length),the element is similar to bool or torch.uint8
